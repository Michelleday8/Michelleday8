ABOUT RAINBOWBRIDGE WORLD

About

Korean record label. It houses girl group MAMAMOO, boy group Vromance, rapper Basick, trio Phantom and duo Geeks among others. The label was founded by Kim Do-hoon, a prominent K-pop producer, Hwang Sung-jin and Kim Jin-woo. Kim Jin-woo originally is the representative director of Cube Entertainment's Music Cube. Kim Jin-woo was in charge of the practice room rental business and then expanded his business with opening AN Bridge, Rainbow Bridge Academy and ENB Academy Practical Music Academy, which integrated under the name Modern & Bridge. In March 2010, he later established the first OEM company, Rainbow Bridge Agency, which operates to train students who are not trainees with similar process as the regular entertainment company, such as vocal, dance, acting, language, etc.RBW is an integrated entertainment company established by Korean top producers including co-CEOs, Kim Jin Woo and Kim Do Hoon. With the aim of creating quality contents for the world, RBW makes new paradigm and becomes the leading enterprise in entertainment business industry

K-POP EDUCATIONAL TRAINING PROGRAM & TOUR

RBW’s K-POP Education Training & Tour Program is a newly developed version of global artist training program. Our new program provides KPOP artist aspirants with opportunities to have the same experiences that famous KPOP artists have gone through before making their debut. This program proceeds in RBW global training center, 400 square meters, equipped with full facilities including recording studio, dance room, ensemble room, accommodations, and fitness rooms inside. Not only the facilities, human resources of RBW, producers, directors, trainers, and A&R staffs specialized in K-POP, will provide the best experience to the participants Global artists who had been through this program have made successful career back in their local. As the center of global artist training business, RBW promises to deliver quality education and training system

Date Event
August 2011 Hwang Sung-jin (former Music Cube producer) and Kim Do-hoon (former Music Cube director) joined Kim Jin-woo's Modern & Bridge and took representative and director positions
March 2012 Kim Do-hoon joined corporation with Rainbow Bridge Agency’s established music label, WA Entertainment.
August 2012 WA Entertainment along with Brand New Music debuted Hip hop trio Phantom.In December 2012, Hip hop duo Geeks signed with WA Entertainment.
June 2014 WA Entertainment debuted their first girl group Mamamoo
August 2014 RBW debuted composer Esna as a solo singer. In September 2014, WA Entertainment along with TSN Entertainment debuted brother duo OBROJECT.
March 2015 WA Entertainment merged with Rainbow Bridge Agency to create an independent label RBW (acronym for Rainbow Bridge World).In September 2015, Yangpa signed with RBW.In December 2015, rapper Basick and Big Tray signed with RBW.
June 2016 Monday Kiz signed with RBW. Later that month, RBW confirmed that rock band M.A.S 0094 joined the label, as well as the changing of the band name to MAS (acronym for Make A Sound). In July 2016, RBW debuted vocal group Vromance.
June 2018 RBW revealed that MAS would re-debut under the name "ONEWE" and RBW BOYZ as "ONEUS"
January 2019 RBW debuted ONEUS.
